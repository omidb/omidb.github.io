CV

I'm on the job market for industry research positions!

I'm interested in building intelligent agents capable of performing human-level intelligent tasks. I am mainly focused on deep natural language understanding, with the ultimate goal of building systems which have inferential abilities for interacting with the world via natural language. These days I work on developing computational models of semantics for understanding different aspects of language. Throughout my undergrad, I did research in Robotics and Multi-agent Systems.
